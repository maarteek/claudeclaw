## Pattern Recognition: What Works vs What Doesn't

**Successes** (automated, impersonal, operator-not-salesperson):
- E-commerce with drop shipping (orders flow through software, no client relationships)
- Facebook fan pages at scale (audience building without 1:1 interaction)
- When traffic was cheap/free (SEO, early Facebook)

**Failures** (selling to resistant people, delivering through contractors, markets with no footprint):
- PPC for solicitors (resistant buyers, poor delivery via contractor)
- Lead gen in water damage restoration (no presence in that market/geography)
- AI database reactivation pitch to Jean (wrong product for the audience)

**The pattern**: Automated delivery beats service delivery. Inbound beats outbound. Being the operator beats being the salesperson. Selling to people who already want to buy beats convincing resistant buyers.
